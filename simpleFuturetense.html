Simple Future Tense

Introduction

The Simple Future Tense is used to describe:

  • Actions that will happen in the future.
  • Predictions or assumptions about the future.
  • Spontaneous decisions (made at the moment of speaking).

Definition

This tense expresses uncertainty or certainty about future events, depending on the context.
